Here we have developed an application which identifies 5 types of skin diseases based on their features namely:

  • Acne-is a skin condition that occurs when your hair follicles become plugged with oil and dead skin cells.
  • Eczema-is a condition wherein patches of skin become inflamed, itchy, cracked, and rough
  • Measles-is a highly contagious, serious disease caused by a virus.
  • Melanoma-is a type of skin cancer that develops from the pigment-producing cells known as melanocytes.
  • Psoriasis-is a skin disorder that causes skin cells to multiply up to 10 times faster than normal. This makes the skin build up into bumpy red patches covered with white scales.
